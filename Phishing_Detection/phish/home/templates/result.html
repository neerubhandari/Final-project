About

BloodBank+

"Imagine a situation where someone close to you is in the ICU. The next thing you hear is a call from the doctor asking you to get AB(-ve) blood as soon as possible. You dash to the nearest blood bank but just to get disappointed. You try out every possible way at your disposal. You are confused, stressed, and afraid all at the same time.” This intimidating situation has become but a common scenario lately.

Each and every one of you can be a part of the venture. All you have to do is register your account and you are good to go. Registration can be done via our website, Mobile App or SMS. Being in our first phase, we'll be collecting data of donors and validating them.

Since we're still working with our platform, we won't however be able to fulfill the blood request via our website (but you can always let us know if you need blood and we'll connect you to the probable donors). By the first or second week of December, 2016, we'll launch the fully functioning system should we have a full-fledged database of Blood Donors by that time.
